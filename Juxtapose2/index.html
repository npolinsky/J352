The Rebuild from Hurricane Katrina

Hurricane Katrina Damage and Recovery Juxtapose

The juxtapose shows the immediate damage following Hurricane Katrina and the rebuilding effort that formed the city a decade later. Adjusted for inflation as of February 2025, Hurricane Katrina caused an estimated $201.3 billion in damages, the costliest tropical cyclone in U.S. history. The federal government spent over $9 billion compensating homeowners that either rebuilt or abandoned their properties.
