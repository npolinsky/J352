Meet Tyler Quattrin

Roots in Minnesota

Tyler Quattrin is a sophomore journalism major from Minneapolis, Minnesota. He writes for The Diamondback. Tyler’s roots in journalism stem from his hometown. Minneapolis was in the national spotlight in 2020 after the tragic murder of George Floyd. During this time, COVID had also ramped up in the United States. Tyler saw the news coverage during this time period, and there were two main reasons journalism became an interest. He felt like informing the public was an important job, and he also felt like it was a fun job having the stage to share important news.

In His Free Time

Despite being from the frigid north, Tyler enjoys swimming, though there are only indoor facilities to his disposal. In his free time he also enjoys puzzling. If he had a super power, it would be the ability to freeze time, so he could pause everything around him and get really smart, allowing him to be ahead of others.

Over winter break, he spent some valuable time with high school friends, and saw a few movies, including the new Marty Supreme. While they were all in high school, this same group would catch a movie every Tuesday.

Tyler hates brussel sprouts, a feeling we had in common.
