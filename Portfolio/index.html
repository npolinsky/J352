About Me

Nick Polinsky is a sports broadcaster and writer who is currently a sophomore at the University of Maryland. He graduated from Archbishop Curley High School in Baltimore, MD, where he first started his play-by-play journey for Curley's broadcast club, "Friars Nation". Nick served as the lead play-by-play broadcaster for Friars' football, baseball and basketball all four years, and after serving as the President of the club for both his upperclassmen years, he won the Friars Nation Award prior to graduation.

Since starting at Maryland, Nick has continued his passion for broadcasting through multiple different student organizations at UMD. He has done on-air broadcasts for Big Ten Plus, WMUC Sports Radio, and Maryland Baseball Network.

In addition to his broadcasting, Nick also writes for two Baltimore sports websites, Russell Street Report and Eutaw Street Report.

If Nick isn't busy with his broadcast or print duties, you can find him watching the Ravens and Orioles, officiating baseball or basketball, or on the beach down in Ocean City, MD.
